About Us

Burhani Education Society

Burhani College is managed by Burhani Education Society, a registered society which was founded by Syedna BurhanuddinRA in 1969 with the aim of “imparting education in all fields and branches of learning and knowledge irrespective of caste, creed, or religion.” The founding of Burhani College continues an over 1400 year history of the Dawoodi Bohra Community.

Our courses cover a Junior College in Commerce and Arts, a Degree College leading to the degrees of Bachelor of Commerce, Bachelor of Arts, Bachelor of Management Science, Bachelor of Arts in Mass Media and Communication and Master of Commerce, and Certificate courses in vital life skills under the Sciences of the Home program.

Our Inspiration

We work towards shaping the minds of our students to become better human beings with a higher intellect for a better tomorrow. We heralded the quiet completion of our 50th year in July 2020, in the midst of the Covid-19 global pandemic. Yet, we continue to be optimistic about our future and remain committed to the cause of knowledge and learning and helping students from all walks of life to achieve their goals in the 21st Century through innovation, leadership & entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

Our vision is encapsulated in one simple phrase : “Knowledge for All”. This is our core guiding principle. It allows us to cater the needs of thousands of students year after year, and encourages us to continue doing the same for generations to come.

Our Mission

  1. To evolve competent and value-oriented leaders by providing them with holistic and contemporary education.
  2. To cater to the academic needs of the community with a keen focus on promoting education for girls and economically marginalized and weaker sections of society.
  3. To impart quality education to students with an emphasis on blending knowledge of culture and tradition with modernity and technology.
  4. Empowering the youth to uphold values of humanity, truth, justice, nationhood, global peace, and harmony.
