Burhani College
Of Commerce And Arts

Established in 1970, Burhani College is a Commerce, Liberal Arts, Management Science and Life Skills College located in the heart of Mumbai, India's largest city and financial capital of the country. We work towards shaping the minds of our students to become better human beings with a higher intellect for a better tomorrow. We heralded the quiet completion of our 50th year in July 2020, in the midst of the Covid-19 global pandemic.

Our Campus

Located in the heart of central Mumbai, amidst what can be called an educational enclave, within walking distance of the Byculla and Dockyard Road train stations, Burhani College has been offering quality education for over 50 years. Our lush green campus is a symbol of our belief in sustainability and provides an environment that fosters the holistic development of mind body and soul.

Our Facilities

Our vision is encapsulated in one simple phrase : “Knowledge for All”. This is our core guiding principle. It allows us to cater the needs of thousands of students year after year, and encourages us to continue doing the same for generations to come. At Burhani College, students not only learn within the four walls of the classrooms but also from their off-classroom interactions with peers and faculty. The cultural diversity in the campus promotes equality, respect and compassion for one and all.

Library

A library is a cornerstone of knowledge. We believe in the words of renowned philosopher Henry Davi Thoreau- “Books are the treasured wealth of the world.” We maintain a library that consists of more than 4,000 textbooks, 26,000 reference books, 79 rare books and 18 journals.

Auditorium

The college has a fully equipped WiFi enabled auditorium. This is utilized for organizing seminars, guest lectures, and meetings. During festival, many events take place here.

Health Services

The college has set up an infirmary for the students, equipped with first-aid medical help. In case of emergencies, we take proceeding actions with consultation of Prince Aly Khan Hospital adjacent to our college.

Courses We Offer

Junior College Courses

Junior college studies constitute the first step that students take towards a career path upon completion of 10th grade in high school. We offer junior college courses in two streams - Commerce and Arts.

Undergraduate Courses

Undergraduate studies help shape the course of the students' crucial college years. We offer undergraduate courses for the Degrees of Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) and Bachelor of Arts (B.A.).

Management Courses

We offer undergraduate management courses for the Degrees of Bachelor of Management Studies (B.M.S.) and Bachelor of Arts in Mass Media and Communications (B.A.M.M.C.).

Post Graduate Courses

For individuals who are more academically oriented, we offer Post-Graduate Degrees of Master of Commerce (M.Com.) and Doctor of Philosophy in Commerce (Ph.D. in Commerce).
