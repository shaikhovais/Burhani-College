Burhani College creates global learning opportunities for students and faculty members as it collaborates with the world-renowned educational institution

Burhani College has entered into an academic collaboration with Harvard Business School Online to offer Harvard Business School Online courses to its students. This collaboration is an extension of Burhani College's commitment for providing multi-disciplinary courses to create world class business leaders and entrepreneurs for tomorrow.

According to Ms. Naheed Ansari, the Academic Director of Burhani College, “Harvard Business School Online offers some of the most enriching business courses in the world, and with this collaboration we will offer significant value addition to our existing courses.”

The focus of Harvard Business School Online courses is to enhance student's proficiency and skillset in various verticals. It offers a unique and highly engaging way to learn vital business concepts through courses like Entrepreneurship Essentials, Sustainable Business Strategies, Business Analytics, Disruptive Strategy, Global Business and Leadership Principles. Its bundled Credentials of Readiness essentials (CORe) course prepares students for mastering the core principles of finance through Business Analytics, Economics for Managers and Financial Accounting - subjects that are the key to developing any good business or profession.

The online certificate and credential programs are offered via HBS Online's innovative online platform and are designed to bring the Harvard Business School classroom experience to life. The HBS Online courses put participants at the centre of the learning experience. The learning model is built around three key characteristics: active, case-based, and social learning. All HBS Online courses are led by industry experts and international faculty and align smoothly with global standards of excellence.
